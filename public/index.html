How to annotate
  1. Read the question and its answer in the top card.
  2. Use the Source · FAQ card at the bottom — the original FAQ this item was synthesized from — as your reference when judging accuracy.
  3. Pick the single best answer from the options — the model names are hidden on purpose.
  4. Rate the test pair using the panel on the right; the fields shown depend on the question (for example, whether the links in the answer are valid). You may open URL ↗ / Source ↗ to verify.
  5. Save & Next records your answer and loads the next item. Use Back / Next to revisit and edit items from this session.
  6. You'll annotate up to 100 items; your progress is shown by the counter at the top right.
